    • ABOUT SEVENTEEN 🎥

    SEVENTEEN is a well-known Korean pop group under the HYBE company. This group consists of thirteen, fun and witty members. They also have a Youtube channel named SEVENTEEN. Their main content is a web series called "Going Seventeen."

    For a proper welcome, here's the main intro audio to Going Seventeen since 2021:

    ABOUT GOING SEVENTEEN: EGO PT.1 and PT.2 🎥

    The "Going Seventeen: EGO" pack is a thriller themed, 2-episodes-deal, Halloween Special. Though horror is the most prominent vibe you may get when you jump into the video, there is definitely a sense of mystery in the series as you watch it closely. Many fans of the series, called Carats (fanbase of the Kpop group, Seventeen, themselves) and Cubics (fanbase of the Going Seventeen web series itself), freely come up with their own theories and expectations in between the two separate release dates of each episode. This gives creative freedom in the minds and in the imaginations of the viewers. It may even stimulate you as some parts may get you thinking of what may come next, or even get your heart racing.
